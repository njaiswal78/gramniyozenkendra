Gram Niyozen

Kendra

GRAM Niyozen Kendra is a humanitarian focal Non-Governmental Organisation with an ambition extend helping hand to the widows and children who are deprived of love and care, books and teachers, food and shelters. General awareness towards health hazards and encouragement of arts and culture are few areas of our concern and in short span of time we have modestly worked in all these areas with tremendous support of well-wishers and sympathisers who have voluntarily donated to fulfil and ambition . We strive to wipe tears from every eye and radiate love & affection wherever we go. We cherish to widen the circle to cover the whole of humanity.

Welcome to Gram Niyozen Kendra

The Organization Gram Niyozen Kendra formed by a group of Gandhiyan thought activist on 02 October 1990 on the leadership of Vinod Kumar Pandey against backwardness,illiteracy,non availability of health facility,caste problem,disparity in community and low condition of rural people.

Harnaut block is a flood front area from 100 years back.Flood water of about 15 river crossing in the border of Harnaut block.These for examine health,education and living condition of rural people is very worst.

In thsi scenario,same youth formed this organization.

Our Programmes/Activities

Environment Awareness

Our society realized government systemcannot control environment pollution,it is only possible with support of community.Awareness programme camp organized our society and actively engaged in implementing various environmental protection and development programmes to mobilize communities.

Skill Developmeent Programme

Our organization has organized Skill Development Programme at Organizational head office campus through own rresources.There are many unemployed male and female youths who have got benefitted with this programme.

Woment Empowerment Programme

Keeping the year of Women Empowerment in a view the organization has conducted a seminar-cum workshop to make the people more positive towards women and child.We have focussed our light towards the women rights,health,education status of women and all other neglecting areas where she was ignored.

Health & Family Welfare Programme

Family welfare programme was started by the society with the objective of reducing the birth rate to the extent necessary to stabilize the population at a level consistent with the requirements of the National Economy.

Rural Development Programme

Society organized many programme for rural development and working for upliftment of the life and livelihood of rural India.It aims to promote a single window delivery need based services in various areas.

Agriculture & Horticulture Dev. Programme

Society made the programme for agriculture & horticulture development to want every farmer to attach with science to increase land fertility and increase rate of crop production on their field.

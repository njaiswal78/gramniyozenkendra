About Us

The Organization Gram Niyozen Kendra formed by a group of Gandhiyan thought activist on 02 October 1990 on the leadership of Vinod Kumar Pandey against backwardness,illiteracy,non availability of health facility,caste problem,disparity in community and low condition of rural people. Harnaut block is a flood front area from 100 years back.Flood water of about 15 river crossing in the border of Harnaut block.These for examine health,education and living condition of rural people is very worst. In this scenario,same youth formed this organization.
